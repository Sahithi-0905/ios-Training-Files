Fish Fry
Ingredients:

2 fish fillets (any firm fish)
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, Â½ tsp coriander powder
Juice of 1 lemon, salt to taste
Rice flour (for coating), oil for frying
Steps:

Marinate fish with ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, and salt for 20 mins.
Coat with rice flour for crispiness.
Shallow fry until golden on both sides.
